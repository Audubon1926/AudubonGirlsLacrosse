About the club

Built by Audubon,
for Audubon.

Audubon Girls Lacrosse started with a few players and one belief — that our town deserves a lax program. No experience required. Just show up.

We're a community-run high school girls lacrosse club open to players in grades 9–12. Whether you've never picked up a stick or played youth ball, there's a spot for you here.

We practice on local fields throughout spring and compete in scrimmages across South Jersey. Players looking for more reps can also suit up with our neighbors at the Haddon Township youth program.

Our goal isn't just to field a team — it's to build something that lasts. Every girl who joins is helping put Audubon lacrosse on the map.

Get involved

Ready to play?
Here's how.

01

Fill out the interest form

Takes about 60 seconds. Tell us your name, grade, and whether you've played before. We'll reach out with everything you need.

02

Come to a practice

No tryouts — just show up. We'll get you a stick if you don't have one and go from there. Practices are on Audubon fields.

03

Play in a scrimmage

Once you've got the basics, you'll be game-ready. Six scrimmages across South Jersey this season.

04

Help us grow the program

Tell your friends. Every new player makes the program stronger — and helps us eventually become a full school team.
